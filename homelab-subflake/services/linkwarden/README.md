# Linkwarden

Self-hosted collaborative bookmark manager for collecting, organizing, and
preserving webpages, articles, and more.

## Overview

Linkwarden archives and stores full snapshots of bookmarked pages (screenshots,
PDFs, readable text, and monolithic HTML), so links remain accessible even after
the original goes offline. Collections can be shared with other users.
